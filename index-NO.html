Welcome

The Bodacious Bazaar is a 3 day shopping extravaganza and festival held twice a year
in the Fall and Spring located in Hampton Virginia.

We are a family owned and operated business. We are community minded with a strong belief that you get back what you put out. Our goal is to bring a quality show to the Hampton Roads Area and to donate a portion of the proceeds to chosen local nonprofits.
