What A Great Location for a Show!

Hampton occupies the center of Costal Virginia a vibrant collection of communities in the heart of the Mid-Atlantic region. Two-thirds of the population living on the East Coast can drive here in a day or less. Attendees enjoy convenient access to more than 200 significant attractions stretching from Williamsburg to Norfolk and Virginia Beach all within a 30 minute drive.
